Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices bread
  • 8 slices bacon
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 slices cheddar cheese
  • Butter

Instructions:

1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.

2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.

3. Place 4 slices of bread butter-side down onto the skillet.

4. Top each slice with 2 slices of cheese, bacon, tomato slices, and another slice of bread, butter-side up.

5. Cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.

6. Serve hot and enjoy!
